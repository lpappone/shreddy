Where To Stay

Please reserve early! Summer is busy in Vermont, and lodging is limited.

Camping

You are welcome (and encouraged!) to camp on-site. Squabetty has a HUGE yard, so there is plenty of space to spread out and set up a tent, or sleep under the stars. We'd love to have the extra hang out time and save you the hassle of traveling back and forth. You can shower/primp for the party in the house, and if it rains, worst case, you can stay dry in the barn or under the tent we'll have set up. If you need to borrow camping gear, let us know.

Other area hotels

Stowe Mountain Lodge: Near the base of Stowe, about 30 minutes from Squabetty, with a variety of room options (though it will fill up quickly). Call 888-478-6938 or reserve online.

Sterling Ridge Cabins: Sterling Ridge is in Jefferson, about 15 minutes from Squabetty, and has some beautiful cabins and views. This would be a great spot for groups to rent together. For instance, their “Wilderness Cabin” has 3 bedrooms and sleeps up to 8 for $350 a night. Call 800-347-8266.

Suzanne's Farm: 15 minutes away in Cambridge, VT, and pet friendly. 802-644-6325.

Smuggler's Notch Inn: Also in Cambridge, with a hot tub! 802-644-6607

Other options

Airbnb, VRBO, Homeaway, etc., have a number of listings nearby. For reference, Squabetty is at 165 Deane Rd. in Underhill, Vermont.
