Getting to Squabetty

On Saturday, we'll have shuttles going from area hotels to Squabetty and back. On Friday, you're on your own. Please carpool! The house is about a mile down a dirt road and it's in a rural area so taxis, ubers, etc., will probably not be an option.

Directions

165 Deane Rd, Underhill, VT 05489

Deane Rd. is off of Pleasant Valley Rd., which runs North-South between Underhill and Cambridge. If you're coming from the South, it will be a sharp right. From the North, it's a left. Deane Rd. is unpaved, so take it slow, and just keep going. And going. There is a parking area on the left side shortly before you reach the house, so please leave your car there and walk up the rest of the road. You can't really get lost because if you go too far, you'll arrive at the house. If you do get lost or need any help, call the land line at 802.899.4491.
